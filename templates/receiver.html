How to receive a file ?

1. Get ID

A friend should have created a pipe already. Get the room id from your friend.

2. Paste ID

Paste the id from your friend in the designated field and press connect (or enter).

3. Wait your peer

Wait until getting "Connected" status. It usually takes a few seconds.

4. Download Files

Files will automatically be downloaded to your device
